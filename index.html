The Mission

In an increasingly digital world, this event intentionally creates space for traditional, manual writing as a powerful tool for self-expression, emotional connection, and creativity.

What it is: A hands-on writing experience designed to reconnect students, families, and community members to pencil, paper, and emotional expression.

Who is invited: Elementary, middle, and high school students, parents, caregivers, and community members.

Vibe Check

This is not a lecture-style event or photo opportunity. It is built to be experienced.

It takes a village

Parents, caregivers, and community members we need you! You too can participate in interactive activities designed to support the gathering and support the growth of our students.
